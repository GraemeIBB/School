Introduction

Wildlife animals are vital to maintaining ecological balance, inhabiting diverse ecosystems like forests, oceans, and grasslands. They face threats from habitat destruction and climate change, making conservation crucial for their survival.

According to COSEWIC's wildlife assessment as of may 2022, there are 841 wildlife species in various risk categories, including 371 Endangered, 196 Threatened, 253 Special Concern, and 21 Extirpated. In addition, 23 wildlife species have been assessed as Extincted.

Here are some ways to protect endangered species

  1. Learn about endagered species in your area
  2. Minimize use of herbicides and pesticides that may lead to amphibian deformities and death
  3. Avoid hunting endangered species for sport
  4. Recycle and buy sustainable products
  5. Volunteer
